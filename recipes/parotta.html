Parotta

Home

a layered flatbread from southern India,made with ghee or oil and usually maida or white flour.

    Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk

    preparation steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, and egg in a bowl.
  2. Dissolve sugar in milk and add it to the flour.
  3. Add oil to the flour and knead for at least five minutes.
  4. Let the dough rest for about an hour.
  5. Knead the dough again for a minute.
  6. Roll out the dough on a flat surface dusted with flour until it's very thin.
  7. Heat oil in a tawa and cook the parotta on both sides.
  8. Fluff up the parotta by gently beating it.
